Fencing

What is Fencing?

Fencing is not actually a single sport but a group of three related combat sports. The three disciplines in modern fencing are the Foil, the Épée, and the Sabre, each which uses a different kind of weapon and has different rules.

In the sport, points are made through weapon's contact with an opponent. Modern fencing uses sophisticated electronic equipment to detect contact.

Fencing was one of the first sports to be played at the Olympics. In fact, competitive fencing is one of the five activities to be featured at every modern Olympic Games, ever since 1896

In Singapore, the national governing body of the sport is Fencing Singapore. There are currently 3000 fencers in the fencing community and there are a variety of local and interntional fencing competitions.
